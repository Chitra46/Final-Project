Overview

Embark with a friend on an unparalleled expedition into the pristine wilderness of Antarctica, a once-in-a-lifetime odyssey marked by sophistication and refinement. Antarctica's breathtaking vistas, from grandiose icebergs to rugged glaciers and serene fjords, call for your exploration. Start your trip in Buenos Aires, Argentina's cosmopolitan capital. Explore the city's diverse neighbourhoods, from the vivacious La Boca – famed for its street tango performances – to Puerto Madero, a rejuvenated waterfront enclave boasting upscale dining. Recoleta awaits, with its elegant cemetery housing the resting place of Eva "Evita" Peron. Then, fly south to Ushuaia. The city's colourful streets and eclectic architecture cascade from imposing peaks to the tranquil shores of the Beagle Channel. Ushuaia fully embraces its 'end of the world' allure, amplified by its dramatic surroundings. Onboard the Swan Hellenic ‘SH Vega’ or ‘SH Diana’ ship, navigate to Antarctica. Seals lay on icy shores, while the remarkable diversity of birdlife dazzles, and breaching whales deliver exhilarating spectacles. Accompanied by seasoned polar experts, you'll explore historic sites, delving deep into the continent's storied past. The ship offers chic spaces for socialising, relaxation and cherishing private moments. Whether you choose to bask in the comfort of your cabin, utilise the ingeniously designed ship's viewing areas, or go up to the Swan's Nest observation deck, ample opportunities abound to marvel at astonishing life and spot lounging leopard seals lounging on ice. Your Antarctic voyage unfolds with a captivating itinerary, encompassing the legendary Drake Passage, encounters with penguin colonies in uninhabited islands, and a mesmerising journey through often iceberg-laden waters en route to the Antarctic Peninsula. Keep your senses alert for breaching whales, majestic albatrosses, and the various seal species that thrive in this frozen realm. Onboard, relish superb service and fine dining, and revel in all the luxuries befitting a five-star cruise. Between unforgettable shore excursions, engage in captivating enrichment presentations hosted by wildlife experts in the ship's state-of-the-art research lab. With return international full-service flights, a return internal chartered between Buenos Aires and Ushuaia, a 9-night Swan Hellenic expedition cruise to Antarctica and 4 nights of 5-star hotel accommodation in Buenos Aires. It's an unbeatable proposition for those wanting to discover the White Continent.
