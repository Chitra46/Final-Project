Overview

Immerse yourself in Japan's captivating beauty and rich culture on this incredible 8-day unbeatable highlights tour. Your journey begins in bustling Tokyo, one of the world's most captivating cities. Discover Tokyo's must-see landmarks, including the serene Meiji Shrine and the Imperial Palace. Next, venture to the awe-inspiring Mt Fuji and admire the breathtaking views from the 5th Station, perched 2300 meters above sea level. Enjoy a short cruise on the serene waters of Lake Kawaguchiko in Hakone, surrounded by picturesque landscapes and the majestic presence of Mt Fuji. In the ancient capital of Kyoto, wander at your leisure and stop by the celebrated Kiyomizudera Temple, a treasure of Japanese architecture. If you desire, join an optional half-day sightseeing tour of Kyoto, where you'll visit the Fushimi Inari Shrine, Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion), and the enchanting Arashiyama Bamboo Grove. Explore Nara Park, where you'll encounter over a thousand free-roaming deer, and visit the famous Todaiji Temple, a symbol of Japan's historical significance.
