Overview

Pop your safari hat on for this trip of a lifetime through Sri Lanka, the jewel of South Asia. A wild and vast landscape of untamed jungles, fragrant tea plantations and lost temples - now you can experience it all in style with this 5-star luxury small group tour. Kick your journey off with a guided tour of Negombo's most significant sites, before journeying through the lush Sigirya, where you can take up the optional tour of the imposing rock fortress. Journey through the time-worn city of Polonnaruwa, where the ancient relics left behind, like the Royal Palace and Lotus Bath, will paint a vivid picture of the emperors and empresses who reigned there centuries ago. The fun doesn't end there, though; you will also take in the gilded majesty of the Sacred Tooth Relic in Kandy, buckle up for a Jeep safari through Yala National Park and enjoy a traditional high tea in the pioneering tea town of Nuwara Eliya and so much more. Feel like Indiana Jones by day before retreating to luxurious 5-star accommodation by night on this incredible tour featuring return international full-service flights, daily breakfast, return airport transfers and more.
