Overview

Discover the ancient marvels of China on an unforgettable 12-day discovery tour, including a chance to see the incredible Terracotta Warriors - a wonder like no other on the planet. Crafted over 2,000 years ago to accompany Emperor Qin Shi Hung into the afterlife, these warriors are a testament to China's rich history and culture. Start your adventure in Beijing, where you'll visit the magical Forbidden City and the iconic Tiananmen Square. Walk along the Great Wall of China, explore the tea plantations at Meijiawu Tea Village, and marvel at the tranquil beauty of Suzhou's Lingering Garden. Admire the stunning skyline of Shanghai from The Bund promenade, and delve into China's fascinating past at the Shanghai Museum. But the highlight of this trip is undoubtedly the chance to witness the Terracotta Army in Xi'an. Be spellbound by this incredible wonder's intricate detail, unique expressions, and sheer scale as you experience it in person. This tour includes everything you need for a comfortable and memorable journey, from return international full-service flights to 10 nights accommodation in 4-star hotels. You'll also have the support of English-speaking tour guides and enjoy the convenience of return airport transfers. Don't miss this once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to discover the wonders of China on a trip that's sure to leave you with unforgettable memories.
